Instructions

  1. Step 1: Soak rice and fenugreek seeds together for 6 hours.
  2. Step 2: Soak urad dal separately for 6 hours.
  3. Step 3: Grind urad dal to a smooth batter, adding water as needed.
  4. Step 4: Grind rice and fenugreek seeds to a coarse batter.
  5. Step 5: Mix both batters, add salt, and let ferment overnight.
  6. Step 6: Pour batter into idli molds and steam for 10-15 minutes.

Requirements

  • Idly Rice: 2 cups
  • Urad Dal (Split Black Lentils): 1 cup
  • Fenugreek Seeds: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Salt: to taste
  • Oil or Ghee: for greasing the idly molds
  • Water: as needed
